# Retention Strategy Report

## 1. Champions
Customers with high spending and recent purchases.

### Strategy
- VIP rewards
- Early product access
- Loyalty points

### Business Value
High revenue contribution and strong brand loyalty.


---

## 2. Loyal Customers
Customers with frequent purchases.

### Strategy
- Subscription offers
- Personalized recommendations

### Business Value
Stable repeat revenue.


---

## 3. Dormant Customers
Customers inactive for long periods.

### Strategy
- Win-back campaigns
- Discount coupons

### Business Value
Can reactivate lost customers.


---

## 4. High Risk Unhappy
Customers with many complaints/support tickets.

### Strategy
- Priority support
- Refund assistance
- Dedicated customer care

### Business Value
Reduces negative brand impact.


---

## 5. Low Value Customers
Low spending customers.

### Strategy
- Budget product bundles
- Small discounts

### Business Value
Potential future growth.


---

# Campaign Budget Prioritization

Priority Order:

1. High Risk Unhappy
2. Champions
3. Loyal Customers
4. Dormant Customers
5. Low Value Customers

Reason:
Saving high-value unhappy customers prevents major revenue loss.
